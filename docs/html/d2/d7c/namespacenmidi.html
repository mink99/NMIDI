◆ ThruMode

enum nmidi::ThruMode
Enumerator
FORWARD_OFF 

Thru Disabled. Nothing received on the MIDI IN port is forwarded to the MIDI OUT port.

FORWARD_OTHER 

Forwards received commands to MIDI OUT unless they're on the listening channel. This makes the most sense, and is the default setting.

FORWARD_SELF 

Forwards received commands to MIDI OUT only if they are on the listening channel.

FORWARD_ALL 

Any traffic received on MIDI IN is immediately forwarded to MIDI OUT. Warning, may cause a feedback loop in some setups!
